Introduction

The Department of Pediatrics is one of the most comprehensive department of this hospital. Complete center for entire pediatrics age group and for infants. We are covering the entire pediatric age group till adolescent, i.e.. Up to 18 years of age. We have got a up graded neonatal ICU, PICU units and adolescent ward, doing all kind of works related to it. We got pediatric physiotherapy and vaccination center. Our newer addition of Neonatal unite is equipped with all monitor, Ventilator, Respirator, Surfactant therapy and all which is unique for the region.
