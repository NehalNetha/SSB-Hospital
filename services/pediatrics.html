Pediatric Care

Compassionate care for children of all ages
by skilled pediatricians in a safe setting.

Overview

Book an Appointment

At Shri Swami Bhumanand Hospital, our pediatric department offers expert medical care tailored to newborns, infants, children, and adolescents in a nurturing and family-friendly environment.

From routine vaccinations and developmental assessments to treating childhood illnesses and emergencies, we focus on your child’s complete physical and emotional well-being.

Services Offered:

Newborn & Infant Care:

  • Comprehensive checkups, growth tracking, and timely vaccinations to ensure healthy early development.
  • Includes: Jaundice care, feeding issues, immunizations.

Common Childhood Illnesses:

  • Diagnosis and treatment for fevers, colds, flu, infections, and seasonal allergies in children.
  • Includes: ENT issues, digestive problems, infections.

Growth & Development Assessments:

  • Regular assessments to monitor mental, emotional, and physical development throughout childhood stages.
  • Includes: Milestone tracking, speech and behavior evaluation.

Emergency Pediatric Support:

  • 24/7 care for urgent medical needs such as asthma attacks, injuries, infections, and high fever.
  • Includes: Emergency stabilization and referral support.

Why Choose Us?

24/7 Emergency Care

We offer round-the-clock emergency services for patients suffering from heart attacks, chest pain, and other critical conditions.
