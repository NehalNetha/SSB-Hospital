About Our Hospital

Shri Swami Bhumanand Hospital (SSBH), established in 2009, is a premier 150-bed super-specialty healthcare institution located in Haridwar, Uttarakhand.

Founded by Anant Shri Vibhushit Swami Achyutanand Tirth Ji Maharaj, the hospital operates under the guiding principle: "Hospital is a Temple; Patient is God; and Doctor is Priest." This philosophy underscores our unwavering commitment to providing exceptional medical care to all individuals, especially the underprivileged.

Our hospital operates 24/7, providing round-the-clock emergency services, pharmacy access, and blood bank facilities to ensure comprehensive care at all times. We also offer 24/7 ambulance services to facilitate prompt medical attention when needed.
