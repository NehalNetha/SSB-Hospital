About Us

Your Health.
Our Commitment

Shri Swami Bhumanand Hospital is a multispecialty hospital providing comprehensive healthcare services to patients in Haridwar and the surrounding areas. Our focus is on delivering exceptional care through expert medical professionals and modern technology.

Our Founder

Shri Swami Bhumanand Hospital was founded by Anant Shri Vibhushit Swami Achyutanand Tirth Ji Maharaj, a visionary spiritual leader whose life was dedicated to selfless service and the betterment of humanity.

With an unwavering belief in the philosophy that "Hospital is a Temple, Patient is God and Doctor is the Priest" Swami Ji envisioned a healthcare institution where compassion and care would transcend barriers of class and privilege. His mission was to create a sanctuary of healing that offers world-class medical care to everyone, especially the underprivileged.

Guided by his spiritual wisdom and deep empathy, Shri Swami Bhumanand Hospital stands as a beacon of hope, transforming lives and inspiring countless individuals to serve humanity with love and dedication. Swami Ji's legacy continues to inspire every aspect of the hospital's work, reminding us that true service lies in uplifting others.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality medical care to all our patients. We are dedicated to using the latest medical advancements to ensure every patient receives the best possible treatment and support.

Our Vision

We aim to be a leading healthcare provider in Haridwar by continuously improving our medical services, adopting cutting-edge technologies, and putting our patients at the heart of everything we do. Our goal is to offer affordable, world-class healthcare to everyone in need.
