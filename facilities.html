Facilities

Facilities

24×7 Emergency

Our 24×7 emergency service provides immediate, life-saving medical care for accidents, trauma, and critical health conditions with rapid diagnosis, expert staff, and advanced equipment available around the clock.

Cath Labs

Cath Labs are specialized facilities equipped for advanced cardiac procedures, including angioplasty and stenting, enabling accurate diagnosis and minimally invasive treatment of heart diseases using real-time imaging and catheter-based technology.

Modular OTs

Modular operation theaters offer state-of-the-art, sterile environments designed for complex surgeries, integrating advanced technology and infection control measures to ensure maximum safety, precision, and efficiency during surgical procedures.

Bronchoscopy

Bronchoscopy is a diagnostic and therapeutic procedure that uses a thin tube with a camera to examine the airways and lungs, detect abnormalities, collect samples, and treat certain respiratory conditions.

Endoscopy

Endoscopy is a minimally invasive procedure using a flexible tube with a camera to visualize the gastrointestinal tract, diagnose digestive disorders, and perform treatments like biopsies, polyp removal, and bleeding control.

Dialysis

Dialysis is a critical treatment for patients with kidney failure, removing waste, excess fluids, and toxins from the blood using advanced equipment, ensuring better health and improved quality of life.

24×7 Pharmacies

Our 24×7 pharmacies ensure uninterrupted access to essential medicines and emergency drugs, supporting continuous patient care and safety with expert pharmacists and computerized inventory systems for accurate dispensing.

Rooms with Modern Amenities

Our hospital rooms offer comfort and care with modern amenities, including air-conditioning, entertainment, nurse call systems, and hygienic interiors, designed to support a peaceful, healing environment for all patients.

24×7 Ambulance Service

Available 24×7, our ambulance service ensures rapid response with GPS-equipped vehicles, trained paramedics, and advanced life-support systems, providing critical care and safe transportation during medical emergencies.
